Why Fraktur began being less used

  1. Italians rediscovered Roman scripts, leading to the creation of Times New Roman.
  2. As Germany united and engaged internationally, Fraktur became difficult for others to read.
  3. Hitler himself opposed it, believing it kept Germany stuck in the past.
